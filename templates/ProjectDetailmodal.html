About this Project

The Kochi Metro Phase 2 Extension marks a significant milestone in Kerala's urban infrastructure development. This ambitious project connects the bustling InfoPark technology hub directly to the city center, addressing critical traffic congestion issues in the Kakkanad region.

Spanning 11.2 kilometers with 11 new stations, the "Pink Line" is designed to facilitate sustainable commuting for over 100,000 daily riders. The project not only enhances connectivity but also integrates advanced green building practices in station construction and solar power utilization for operations.
